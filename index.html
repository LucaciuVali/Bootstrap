About us

Hello and welcome to the change of your life! Our gym is now present in your city and ready to greet you with modern equipment, highly trained personnel and a good time on your road to becoming the person you want to be. We are here, glad to assist you.Why choose our gym? We have invested a great amount of resources of every kind to deliver you a multipractical gym, a great location and the best services you could expect. Our staff is aimed at giving you, the client, the best experience possible while interacting with our gym.

Our story

Our story begins with a burning passion for a healthy life. Years of studying, taking part in many exchange programs, trainings only so we can share all that we learned. All of the people from our staff have one thing in common: we enjoy helping eachother. This affirmation is valid both internally, on a peer-to-peer level, but also on the trainer-client relationship. Our gym is greeting you with the latest technology in the fitness world, independent locker rooms for the ladies and for the gents, each with its own sauna, an indoor and an outdoor pool, massage rooms and private parking for our clients only. Premium clients only get 24/7 access.
